Optimal Entry/Exit Window — Research Plan

Created: 12 March 2026

Goal: Find the daily time window where BTC makes the biggest peak move per hour of exposure, to minimize theta cost relative to take-profit probability.

Key Insight

We buy a straddle/strangle at entry time and hold until take-profit is hit (BTC moves $X up or down from entry). If not hit, we hard-close at exit time. Theta costs us per hour. So we want the window with the biggest max excursion per hour of hold time.

Key Metric: Max Excursion

For a window from H_open to H_close:

  S_entry   = open price of the H_open candle
  MaxHigh   = max(high of each 1h candle in [H_open, H_close-1])
  MinLow    = min(low of each 1h candle in [H_open, H_close-1])
  Excursion = max(MaxHigh - S_entry, S_entry - MinLow)

This is the furthest BTC traveled from entry in either direction —
the number that determines whether take-profit gets hit.

Data Source

Binance BTCUSDT Perpetual Futures — 1h OHLC candles
Endpoint: GET https://fapi.binance.com/fapi/v1/klines?symbol=BTCUSDT&interval=1h&limit=672
No authentication required. Returns: [open_time, open, high, low, close, volume, ...]
672 candles = 4 weeks × 24 hours.

Analytical Steps

Step 1: Build the Triangular Max-Excursion Table

A 24×24 upper-triangular matrix.

  • Rows = entry hour (0–23 UTC)
  • Columns = exit hour (entry+1 through entry+12)
  • Filter: Mon–Fri only (by UTC day-of-week of entry candle)
  • ~20 weekdays of data

For each weekday, for each (entry, exit) pair:

  1. S_entry = open of the entry-hour candle
  2. Scan all candles from entry through exit-1
  3. MaxHigh = max(high of each candle)
  4. MinLow = min(low of each candle)
  5. Excursion = max(MaxHigh - S_entry, S_entry - MinLow)

Average across ~20 weekdays → one number per cell.

Step 2: Build the Efficiency Table (Move Per Hour)

Same shape. Each cell = Avg Excursion / Window Hours.

  Efficiency(H_open, H_close) = Avg Max Excursion / (H_close - H_open)

Core answer: where do you get the most move per hour of theta paid?

Step 3: Identify Optimal Windows

Rank the top 10 (entry, exit) pairs by efficiency. The winner says: "Open at X:00, close at Y:00 — you get $Z of max excursion per hour of hold time."

Constraints

  • Mon–Fri only (filter by UTC day-of-week of entry candle)
  • Min window: 1 hour, max window: 12 hours
  • Entry hours: 0–23 UTC (full 24h coverage)
  • 4 weeks back from today = ~20 weekdays
